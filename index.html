Welcome to Entertin Municipal City Center

Entertin city center is a small community-driven co-operative in Washburn County, Wisconsin.

About Our County

Entertin is a small town in the upper Wisconsin county of Washburn. The town is served by a voluntary county board who supports operations and local collaborations between neighboring cities.

The county board oversees a tri-county fire department, veteran policy force and collection of social and city service organizations culled from Enterin's citizen population.

We hold true to our town motto "Foisons Exeunt"

City Government and Operations

Departments

The Department of Administration is headed by the Mayor as Chief Executive of the City of Entertin and the City Administrator who is the Chief Operating Officer of the City of Entertin. They work jointly to effectively coordinate services that are responsive to citizen needs. City Administration works with the Common Council to identify and solve problems and develop guidelines for municipal operations and services.

Services

The city of Entertin is part of a larger partnership in the tri-city area of Washburn County. City services include police and volunteerfire departments,public transportation, city planning, licensesand applications, parks and recreation, the city council and the library services consortium shared between community members in the tri-city area.

Business

Entertin has a volunteer BID (Business Improvement District)sub-commitee organized by the city aldermen, they meet on Wednesdays at the municipal center between 6-7pm CE/DT.
